AP US HISTORY REVIEW

AP US History is one of the hardest AP class and test with a 50.9% probability of getting passing (3+) score as of 2017. The website will review several essential topics from the AP exam and prepare them for the multiple choice section of the exam through practice questions (quiz) at the end of each topic. This can both serve as last-minute review or long-term extensive lessons on topics for AP US History, better preparing students for the exam in the coming week.

Structure of the Test

The AP U.S. History Exam is 3 hours and 15 minutes long and has a multiple choice/short answer section and a free response section. Each section is divided into two parts.
